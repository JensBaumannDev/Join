Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Join collects, uses, and protects your information. As a student group project developed at the Developer Akademie, this application is for educational purposes and is not intended for commercial use.

1. Data Collection

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use Join, such as:

  • Name and initials
  • Email address
  • Contact information
  • Task-related data and assignments

Additionally, technical information like your IP address, browser type, and operating system may be collected automatically to ensure the functionality of the application.

2. Data Storage and Supabase

Join uses Supabase, a cloud-based database service, to securely store and manage your data. This includes your user profile, tasks, and contacts. By using this application, you agree that your data will be stored on Supabase servers.

3. Purpose of Processing

The data is processed solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing and managing the task management features of Join
  • Educational evaluation of the project by the Developer Akademie
  • Improving the user experience of this prototype

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. All data entered is used exclusively within the scope of this student project. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security as this is a learning project.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data stored about you
  • Request the deletion of your data
  • Object to the processing of your data

Since your data is stored in our Supabase database, you can request its deletion at any time by contacting us or using the provided account management features.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the students listed in the Legal Notice.
